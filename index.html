    Hello! my name is Ayomide Olafusi, a Nigerian-American student from Prince George's County, Maryland. I am currently a sophomore at Norfolk State University, as a cybersecurity major in the DNIMAS (Dozoretz National Institute of Mathematics and Applied Sciences) Honors Program. I plan to use my degree to pursue a career as a Cyber Analyst. I enjoy working in team settings to solve the world's problems in a vast majority of ways, utilizing different tools of technology. At MITRE, my team and I explored and experienced Binary Exploitation, a key aspect of offensive security, focusing on identifying and manipulating vulnerabilities in binary code. At my campus, I hold different positions like Mr.NSBE (National Society of Black Engineers), e-board at NSU's Cybersecurity Club, and more. In my free time, I enjoy going to the gym, music, fashion, shopping, and cooking.
